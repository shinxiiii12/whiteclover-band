About Us

WHITECLOVER is a vibrant fusion band based in Taguig, dedicated to exploring diverse musical styles. Our journey began as a group of friends who shared a passion for music, and we decided to unite our talents to create something unique. With each performance, we blend various genres, inviting our audience to experience a fresh sound. Members include:

  • Darwin Manuel - Lead Vocals
  • Shin Sisgon - Rhythm Guitar
  • Kirby Gutierezz - Bass Guitar
  • Val Pinuela - Lead Guitar
  • Justin Aguilo - Drums
